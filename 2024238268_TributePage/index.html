Tun Dr. Mahathir Mohamad

The longest-serving Prime Minister of Malaysia

Tun Dr. Mahathir Mohamad, a key figure in Malaysia’s development and modernization.

Here's a timeline of Tun Dr. Mahathir Mohamad's life:

  • 1925 - Born in Alor Setar, Kedah
  • 1953 - Graduates as a medical doctor from University of Malaya
  • 1964 - Becomes Member of Parliament
  • 1981 - Becomes the 4th Prime Minister
  • 1980s–1990s - Drives modernization and economic growth
  • 2003 - Retires after 22 years
  • 2018 - Returns as 7th Prime Minister at age 92
  • 2020 - Steps down from office
"I believe that every leader has a right to implement his own policy. But when I see things that are done that are not right - abuse of power, wrong approaches, wrong strategies, making use of foreign consultants, including those discarded by other countries - I feel that I have to have my say."

Tun Dr. Mahathir transformed Malaysia into a modern industrial nation. His leadership focused on infrastructure, economic growth, and national identity.

He remains one of the most influential leaders in Malaysian history.
